BritGeoThermal

BritGeothermal is a new UK based consortium established as a focus for deep geothermal energy research both in the UK and globally. The consortium members include the British Geological Survey and the Universities of Durham, Glasgow and Newcastle. The consortium has the two most recent deep geothermal wells drilled in the UK as part of its portfolio and welcomes collaboration with other research groups and organisations working in the field of deep geothermal energy.
